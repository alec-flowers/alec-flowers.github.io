  About Me  


The first question I usually get asked is how did I end up at a school in Switzerland?

I am actually part Swiss, part American. I grew up in California but visited my grandparents in the italian-speaking part of Switzerland most summers. After I graduated college I had a yearning inside of me to explore the other half of my heritage, to learn a swiss language, and to live in a different place besides California. Professionally, I had decided that I wanted to become an engineer and to meaningful contribute to a product that would improve people's lives. These two desires collided and have lead to me living in Lausanne, learning French and German, and studying to become a Data Scientist at one of the two Swiss Federal Institutes of Technology.


The second question is, how tall are you?

I usually answer this with the nice round number of 200 cm, even though without shoes, the truth is, I am only 198 cm.
