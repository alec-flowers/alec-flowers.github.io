Working: Researching, Interviewing, Writing by Robert A. Caro
My rating: 5 of 5 stars

This small glimpse into how Caro wrote his masterpieces is very inspiring. While some of this was pulled from old interviews or articles, most of it was new to me.

I realized that Caro is not only a great writer, but also master interviewer and researcher. I almost think his research, his compulsion to track down anyone involved, to learn every detail, to know the facts, to re-interview over and over again, to move for 3 years to a new place for his book, is even more impressive than the writing itself.

I took away two main points:
1) you have to turn every page
2) to make the readers see, you have to really see
